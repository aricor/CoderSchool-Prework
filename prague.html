Vi's travel blog (to Czech Republic)

Prague Castle

I first visited here when I was on my way to buy a stamp. Didn't expect google maps to lead me to this place. There's literally a post office inside the castle! For anyone wanting to get to this by tram, get on the 22.

For more info: Prague Castle (Wikipedia)

Charles Bridge

Celebrated New Year, drank champagne, and watched fireworks right on this bridge. Freaking crowded tho.

For more info: Charles Bridge (Wikipedia)
